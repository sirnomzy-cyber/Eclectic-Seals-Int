Our Story

From A Small Team To A Trusted Name In Abuja

Eclectic Seals Int’l was founded on a simple conviction: that the buildings and interiors people live and work in shape the quality of their daily lives. What began as a small team of designers and builders in Abuja has grown into a full-service company spanning interior design, construction, renovation, landscaping, paint manufacturing, and property management.

Every stage of that growth has been guided by the same standard — quality craftsmanship, honest communication, and a refusal to hand over anything less than our best work. We measure ourselves not by the number of projects completed, but by how many clients return to us for their next one.

“We don’t simply construct buildings — we create environments where people live, work, grow, and thrive.”
