Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your name, phone number, email address, and project details submitted through our contact form, quotation request form, or newsletter sign-up.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to remember your preferences, such as dark mode settings, and to understand how visitors use our site. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

Contact Forms

Information submitted through our contact and quotation forms is used solely to respond to your enquiry and, where relevant, prepare a project quotation.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, provide quotations, deliver services you have requested, and, if you opt in, send newsletter updates about our work.

Data Protection

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

We may use third-party services such as Google Maps and email delivery providers to operate our website. These providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

Email Subscriptions

If you subscribe to our newsletter, you can unsubscribe at any time using the link provided in any email we send.

Analytics

We may use analytics tools to understand website traffic and improve our content. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

Your Rights

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

For questions about this policy, contact us at eclecticseals@gmail.com.
